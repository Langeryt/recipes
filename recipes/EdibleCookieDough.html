Edible Cookie Dough

Edible cookie dough is a delectable twist on the classic cookie experience, offering the joy of indulging in raw cookie dough without the worry of raw eggs. This delightful treat features a velvety, buttery base laced with brown sugar and vanilla, creating a rich and satisfying flavor reminiscent of traditional cookie dough. What makes it extra special is the addition of safe-to-eat chocolate chips or chunks, providing delightful bursts of sweetness in every spoonful. Whether enjoyed straight from the bowl or rolled into bite-sized portions, edible cookie dough offers a guilt-free way to savor the irresistible taste of cookie batter, making it a tempting and comforting treat for those with a sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ cup milk chocolate chips
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms. Stir in milk until dough is just combined; fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
